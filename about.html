About Our Journey

At Glow by Beauty, our journey began with a passion for creating cosmetics that bring out the natural beauty in every individual. We started as a small team of beauty enthusiasts with a vision to offer high-quality products that cater to all skin types and tones.

The contemporary Indian beauty expert - Glow by Beauty, continuously innovates to offer a wide range of high performance and world class cosmetics and skincare products. We, at Glow by Beauty, combine international cosmetic technology with an in-depth understanding of the Indian woman’s needs and offer a comprehensive beauty experience to our customers through products that are ideal for a variety of Indian skin tones. Our bond with beauty and fashion goes beyond skincare & makeup hence it is manifested through the renowned Glow by Beauty Fashion Week, which is now the largest fashion event of its kind in the country that celebrates the beauty of fashion.

Join us in celebrating beauty's journey through time. Explore our collection, discover looks that resonate with your mood and the season, and embrace the confidence that comes from knowing you're perfectly aligned with the spirit of every time of the year.
